Welcome!

Andy Beck is a PhD candidate in the department of Biostatistics at the University of Michigan School of Publich Health. His research interests lie in the intersection of Genomics, Machine Learning, and Statistics. Examples of past work and projects can be found here.

Prior to his PhD studies, Andy earned a BS in Mathematics and an MS in Applied Statistics at Loyola University Chicago, followed by an MS in Biostatistics at the University of Michigan. Prior to beginning his PhD studies, Andy worked as a Software Developer at Epic Systems in Verona, WI.
